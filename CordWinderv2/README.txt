# Summary

Winder for Wire, Cord, String.

Features:

*Notches to hold a thick Hair-Tie to wrap around the cord to hold it secure.
*Ring to hold when unwinding wire or cord.
*Designed to do figure 8 winding to eliminate tangles, and to facilitate easy unwinding,
*V shaped holes for BNC Connectors for wire antennas.
